# Project-local templates

Project-local refer to templates that are embedded directly in a site's root. These are functionally identical to proper themes, but take precedence over themes.

Project-local templates can either be used instead of a theme, or to override parts of (or an entire) theme.


* **Layout folder:** `<project root>/_templates`
* **Partials folder:** `<project root>/_partials`

## Writing templates

Writing templates is identical in both themes and project-local templates. A small guide can be found in [Writing templates](Writing templates.md)
